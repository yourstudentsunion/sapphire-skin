Manifesto Points

Maximising the student experience is something that I strive for every day as Athletics and Cross Country Club Captain and as a member of the Students’ Union Sports Committee. I believe that as Your Students’ Union President, I can bring a balanced, approachable and professional manor to the role, making your campus, a happy campus.

As Students’ Union President I will look to:

  • Integrate recreational opportunities from sports clubs, societies and academic fields to enrich a happy university experience for all
  • Enhance engagement between FCH, Park, Oxstalls, Hardwick and Pitville to develop the community spirit and create a circle of safety, leading to a happier university
  • Increase communication so students are more aware of opportunities during enrichment and degree plus weeks, as well as working with the Education Officer to solve timetabling issues

Combining both skills from my criminology degree and Students’ Union activity, I am well placed to represent all aspects of the student voice at the University of Gloucestershire.

In my capacity as Athletics and Cross Country Club Captain and as a member of the Students’ Union Sports Committee I have had a positive impact on university sport; giving me confidence that as President I can lead a team that will have a further positive and far reaching impact on the student experience.
